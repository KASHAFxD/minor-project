Where Did These Values Came From ?

  • Age = (your birth year - current year)
  • Gender = Male (if X & Y chromosome) / Female (if X & X chromosome)
  • Chest Pain = Aypical Angina(predictable) / Atypical Angina (unpredictable) / Non-Anginal (chest pain not related to heart)
  • Resting Blood pressure = Blood pressure while resting
  • Cholestrol = Serum cholestrol level (in mg/dl)
  • Fasting Blood Sugar = blood test after atleast 8h of fasting
  • Resting ECG = Normal / ST-TAbnormality (abnormality in ST segment) / Left Ventricular (Enlargement of Left Ventricle)
  • Maximum Heart Rate = Number of Time Your Heart Beats in a Minute
  • Old Peak = Difference in ST segment Depression Relative to Rest
  • ST Slope = Slope of ST Segment During Exercise
